About Activity Tracker

Activity Tracker is a web application designed for people to quickly and easily record their daily activities. Each entry is saved in a personal history, allowing users to review, update, or delete their activities at any time.

In addition, administrators have tools to view all activities recorded by users, making management and real-time tracking much easier.

Main Features

  • 📌 Record activities with name, duration, and description.
  • ✏️ Edit and update any recorded activity.
  • 🗑️ Delete activities you no longer need.
  • 👀 Administrators can view all activities from all users.
